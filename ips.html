JiuFen old Street

For Social Studies, I analysed the streets of JiuFen old street. I saw that the people were keeping the streets clean and relatively smooth for an old street, and even though the street was located on a mountain, a lot of people had jobs there. I asked a person there and he said he said that he doesn't live in the old street dispite the old street being located on a mountain. And when we tried to leave the man a tip he refused, suggesting a conservative culture where people don't really tip others or they just refuse to accept tips.
